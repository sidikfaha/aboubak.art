Jack Westin is a comprehensive MCAT preparation platform trusted by thousands of medical school aspirants. The platform specializes in CARS (Critical Analysis and Reasoning Skills) and science sections with original practice passages.

## Platform Features

- Daily free MCAT passages delivered to user inboxes
- Comprehensive Question Bank with topic-based filtering
- Personalized study plans and progress tracking
- Flashcards for quick review sessions
- Detailed analytics on performance by topic area

## Learning Methodology

The platform emphasizes critical thinking and fundamental science knowledge - the perfect blend required to excel on the MCAT. All materials are designed to mirror the actual exam format and difficulty level.

## My Role

Developed frontend components using Vue.js, implemented interactive question interfaces with real-time feedback. Built analytics dashboards for student progress tracking. Optimized platform performance for high-traffic periods during exam seasons.
