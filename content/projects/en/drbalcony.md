DrBalcony is a comprehensive inspection services platform helping California property owners comply with SB326 and SB721 balcony safety laws. The platform leverages AI technology to streamline inspections and deliver faster, more accurate results.

## Compliance Services

- SB326 Inspections for condominiums and HOAs
- SB721 Inspections for apartment buildings
- Section 604 Inspections for San Francisco properties
- Multiple property portfolio management
- Engineered repair design services

## Platform Features

- Free instant cost calculator for property estimates
- Online project tracking from inspection to completion
- AI-enhanced inspection accuracy
- Quick report turnaround with detailed findings
- Statewide coverage across all California cities

## My Role

Led frontend development of the inspection management dashboard. Implemented React components for project tracking, report visualization, and client communication. Integrated AI-powered image analysis features for preliminary damage assessment.
