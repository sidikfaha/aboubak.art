SchoolGood is an intensive English learning platform designed specifically for French-speaking students in Africa. It combines live interactive courses with personalized coaching to deliver rapid language proficiency improvements.

## Learning Experience

- 10 live 1-hour courses per week with native English teachers
- Weekly private coaching sessions for personalized feedback
- Unlimited access to premium video, text, and quiz content
- Weekly oral and written challenges with personalized corrections
- Private community for peer support and daily practice

## Platform Features

- Real-time video conferencing for live classes
- Progressive difficulty levels with certification at each stage
- Interactive quizzes and assessments
- Mobile-responsive design for learning on any device

## My Role

Led frontend development, implementing the React-based learning interface with real-time features. Integrated WebRTC for live video classes and Stripe for subscription payments. Optimized performance for users with varying internet speeds.
