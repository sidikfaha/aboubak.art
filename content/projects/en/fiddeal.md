Fiddeal reinvents customer loyalty for local businesses in Africa. The platform replaces traditional paper punch cards with a seamless digital experience accessible via smartphone.

## For Consumers

- Unique QR Code identifier for instant recognition
- Digital wallet for all loyalty cards in one place
- Real-time points tracking and reward notifications
- Quick 30-second registration with phone number

## For Merchants (Fiddeal Pro)

- Ultra-fast QR code scanner for customer identification
- Automatic CRM with customer segmentation
- Automated reward calculations and alerts
- "Ghost accounts" to save points for customers without the app

## Technical Highlights

Architected as a scalable PWA with offline capabilities. Implemented real-time synchronization using Firebase. Built a sophisticated analytics engine for merchant insights.
